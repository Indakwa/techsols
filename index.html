About Us

Who are Tecsols Limited

Tecsols is limited company incorporated in Kenya in the year 2016. The company is located in Nakuru along Nakuru-Nyahururu Road approximately 5 km from Nakuru Town Centre. The focus of Tecsols Limited is to provide Innovative Engineering Technological Solutions in Agricultural (production, post-harvest and storage technologies), Renewable energy (use of solar energy in refrigeration, air conditioning and drying), Water (Solar water desalination and pumping).

Our Services

Explore Our Services

Solar Water Desalination.

Engineering solar-powered water desalination systems that provide clean drinking water for communities in areas with limited fresh water sources.
Post-Harvest Handling Technologies.

Engineering development of innovative post-harvest handling technologies that reduce food waste and improve the quality and shelf life of agricultural products.
Solar-Powered Refrigeration.

Installation of solar-powered refrigeration systems that provide reliable and sustainable cooling for businesses, especially those in the agricultural and food industries.
Solar-Powered Drying.

Design and implementation of solar-powered drying technologies that reduce moisture content in agricultural products, increasing their shelf life and value.
Smart Farming Technologies.

Implementation of smart farming technologies that use sensors and data analytics to optimize crop growth and maximize yield while minimizing resource consumption.
Sustainable Water Management.

Design and implementation of sustainable water management solutions that optimize water usage and reduce waste for communities and businesses.
